About Me

Hi, again! My name is Andrea Amba-an and I'm a Game Developer. My journey into tech started at a very young age. My father, an expert in computers and a passionate gamer, ignited my own love for gaming. At the age of 6, I played my first online game, Flyff. From then on, I explored nearly every online game that became popular in the Philippines, which fueled my dream of creating my own game someday. However, achieving one's dream is never simple, and staying on track has been a challenge. Along the way, I learned and grew in Network Administration, with guidance from my father. Through his mentorship and my experiences, I’ve developed a broad skill set in technology. Thanks to my alma mater, the College of Information and Computing Studies (CICS) at Northern Iloilo State University (NISU), I’ve been brought closer to my dream of becoming a Game Developer.
